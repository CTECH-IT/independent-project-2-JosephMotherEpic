About

Free Geek is a non-profit organization that strives to help bridge the Digital Divide and reduce E-waste.

What is the Digital Divide?

As the world gets more connected to the internet, many people may not have access to an internet connected computer or have the knowledge to use it. Free Geek uses the donations of the broken computers and salvages their working parts to build low-cost comptuers for their thrift shop. Volunteers are taught about computer hardware and software as they go on the next volunteer level. Volunteers can earn thrift store credit to buy a computer. The thrift store sells internet capable computers for as little as $40.
